New!

Organize your signs during your section scan!

During your section scan, you can now add headers to neatly organize signs directly from your dolphin!

While you're doing your section scan, just enter or scan one of the UPCs found here.

For example, you can add a label saying "Aisle 1" by entering 201 into the UPC box.

Instructions for Section Scan Load w/ QuickSign

After you complete your section scan load, press the "Go to Bulk Print" button at the bottom.

Then, select the size you want to print first.

You can organize your signs by pressing the Add Section Label button which will add a new sign with whatever label you want during the print process. These can also be added during the section scan using the codes found above.

You can delete signs by pressing the X button in the top right of each sign.

You can make some last minute changes to the item description and 'WHEN YOU BUY' texts just by clicking on them.

When you're ready, load up the printer, press print, then the signs will start printing!

When you're done with one size, you can press the Switch to (size) button to print the other size.
