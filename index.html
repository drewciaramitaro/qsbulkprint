Instructions

After you complete your section scan or uploading mobile signs, go back to the QuickSign home page and click the "Bulk Print (size)" button at the top of the page.

Then, press "Load Signs".

You can organize your signs by pressing the Add Section Label button which will add a new sign with whatever label you want during the print process.

You can also delete signs by pressing the X button in the top right of each sign.

When you're ready, load up the printer, press print, then the signs will start printing!

When you're done with one size, you can press Back to QuickSign and repeat the above steps with the other size.
